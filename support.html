Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my Mornella Pro purchase?

Open the app, go to Settings or the Pro screen, and tap "Restore Purchases". Your purchase is tied to your Apple ID — make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used to purchase.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Go to Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions on your iPhone, find Mornella, and tap "Cancel Subscription".

I was charged but my Pro features aren't unlocked.

Try tapping "Restore Purchases" in the app. If that doesn't work, restart the app and try again. Still having issues? Email us and we'll sort it out.

Does Mornella work offline?

Yes — affirmations are stored on your device and available offline. Audio playback requires an internet connection to stream.

How do I request a refund?

Refunds for App Store purchases are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com to request a refund.
