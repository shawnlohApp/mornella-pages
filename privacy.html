Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Mornella ("we", "our", or "the app") is a morning affirmation app developed by Shawn Loh. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

Mornella is designed to work without requiring you to create an account or provide personal information. We do not collect your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information directly.

When you purchase a Mornella Pro subscription or lifetime access, your payment is processed securely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We do not receive or store your payment card details. We use RevenueCat to manage and verify your in-app purchase entitlements. RevenueCat associates a pseudonymous, app-generated identifier with your purchases; no personal data is shared with RevenueCat beyond what is necessary for purchase verification.

Automatically Collected Data

To keep the app stable, we collect anonymous crash and error diagnostics — such as device model, operating-system version, app version, and technical error details — using Sentry, a third-party error-monitoring service. This is used solely to identify and fix bugs and is not used to identify you personally.

Ambient Audio

The optional ambient sounds are bundled inside the app and played locally on your device. No audio is streamed from us and no listening data is collected or transmitted.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit: your name or email, your location, your contacts, photos or camera data, microphone recordings, browsing history, or any sensitive personal data. The affirmations you view and save remain on your device.

Third-Party Services

Apple App Store / Google Play: All purchases are processed by Apple or Google under their respective privacy policies (Apple, Google).

RevenueCat: Used to manage in-app purchase entitlements. See their Privacy Policy.

Sentry: Used for crash and error diagnostics. See their Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

Mornella is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Data Retention

We do not maintain a user database and therefore hold no personal data on our own servers that we could delete on your behalf. Crash and diagnostic data collected through Sentry is retained for 90 days and then automatically purged. Purchase entitlement records held by RevenueCat are retained for as long as your account with them exists; you may contact RevenueCat directly to request deletion. Any data stored on your device (such as saved affirmations) can be removed at any time by uninstalling the app.

Governing Law

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at mornella@recnflow.com.
